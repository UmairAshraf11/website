About Us

Introduction

Founded in 1986,UMAIR MALL is an indigenous Pakistani brand. It is ISO certified and has a clear vision of catering to the footwear needs of the community. UMAIR MALL over the years, has established its reputation as one of the finest brands in fashion footwear and accessories all across the country and beyond borders, showcasing a large variety of casual, seasonal, formal, fancy and bridal collections in ladies shoes, clutches, handbags and men’s shoes, keeping in perspective the varied communal likes, interests and aspirations. Metro imports, as well locally produces, a pleasingly diverse line of distinctive designs that comprehensively caters to market sentiments. UMAIR MALL has become one of the first shoe brands that women could turn to for feminine style they coveted and comfort they thought was impossible to attain. With rich leathers and unique details, Metro Shoes has captured the essence of modern femininity… whether that means taking a leisurely walk or running a board meeting. UMAIR MALL pampers your feet with the cushioned, contoured foot bed and experience all-day comfort! The best service, the best price, and the best design, all over the world.

Mission

Our mission is to be the biggest and the best leader in Pakistan’s footwear industry by offering trendy, elegant and ophisticated shoes with tremendous quality, superior customer services and highly modern techniques.

Core Values

Excellence:

Our Vision is to strive for continuous improvement, taking timely & quality decisions and making affirmative contribution towards the achievement of our goals thus responding effectively to customer needs and being able to grasp the hearts of our valued customers.

Creativity:

UMAIR MALL stresses on innovation. We have to come up with new dazzling ideas and novel modifications to satisfy our customers to the fullest.

Quality:

This is one of the critical components. A culture of integrity has to start at the top and can be seen in the conduct and activities of employees by promoting ethical business environment.

Teamwork:

Teamwork supports the achievements of company and helps achieve team goals in a long run.

Unique Store Concepts:

Each and every outlet of Metro is carefully planned and designed keeping in view the essential distinctive mood of the brand as well as the exclusive needs of the customer in that particular vicinity to indulge in their fashion aspirations.
